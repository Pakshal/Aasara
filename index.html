Digitally Connecting Jainism

AASara is a community-driven initiative with a vision to digitally provide services for the Jain community. Our mission is to connect Jain values, compassion, and seva with modern technology. Through digital platforms, awareness initiatives, and community collaboration, we aim to bring Jainism closer to people’s daily lives.

Our Initiatives

1. Feeding Stray Cows

This initiative focuses on collecting rotis from devotees and feeding stray cows on the streets. We place stalls in temples where people can donate rotis, which are later distributed to cows in need.

Many cows are abandoned once they stop giving milk, and male cows are often left on roads without care. This initiative promotes compassion and ensures that these animals receive food and dignity. It also encourages community participation in Jeevdaya and ahimsa.

2. Jain Companion App

We are building a Jain Companion chatbot where users can ask questions related to Jainism on a daily basis.

  • Navkarshi time
  • Pachkan guidance
  • Jain train food assistance using train number
  • Scan food products to check Jain compatibility
  • Bhojanshala in Bangalore
  • Temples in Bangalore
  • Many more features coming soon...

This initiative aims to digitally empower the Jain community and bring essential Jain resources into one accessible platform.

3. Pledge for Shatrunjay

A digital awareness initiative to preserve the sanctity of Shatrunjay Giriraj. The pledge encourages devotees to maintain cleanliness, avoid plastic waste, and respect the sacred nature of the pilgrimage.
